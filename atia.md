## Sign up to test our new accessible CS curriculum

We're developing a new computer science curriculum specifically designed for students using assistive technology. We're looking for volunteers to test our materials.

[**Sign up to be involved in testing our new curriculum with AT users**](https://zeroday.camp/volunteers-needed-accessible-cs-curriculum-testers/)

## Existing resources for micro:bit block coding with eye gaze

These resources give access to existing platforms like micro:bit MakeCode for use with eye gaze and other assistive technologies.

[**Existing resources for using micro:bit MakeCode with eye gaze**](https://kmcnaught.co.uk/block-coding/)

While these existing resources help make current platforms accessible, they have a lot of prerequisites in terms of teacher and student confidence and familiarity with the technology. That's why we're developing a 'from the basics' curriculum with our own simpler bespoke app - which is what we're looking for testers for (see above).
